Encore Guitars

The concept for Encore Guitars originally came from a class in Art Direction, which grouped students of different programs together with the objective of generating the concept of a company, its brand, and several extensions of the brand. I changed the look and feel from the original group project for my portfolio; however the concept is the same in that it is a guitar composed of recycled materials.

Media

Textures created with acryllic paint, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, CoverAction Templates
